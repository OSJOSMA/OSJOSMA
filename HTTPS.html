About OSJOSMA

Founded in 2006 by Oles Andre Etienne and Vandueren Frans, OSJOSMA was established to bridge critical systemic gaps facing orphaned and vulnerable children in Saint-Michel de l'Attalaye. Over two decades, the initiative has transitioned from a localized sanctuary into an organized structural framework integrating primary education and community infrastructure support.

"Every child deserves a safe haven, steady nourishment, and the powerful tool of education. Operating from our main home on Rue Calvaire, we serve as a beacon of hope."

— Oles Andre Etienne, CEO

Our Core Pillars

1. Food Security

Providing structured daily nutritional programs to eradicate childhood hunger and ensure healthy physical growth for all residents.

2. Quality Education

Supplying continuous primary schooling, books, educational materials, and skilled teaching staff to break cycles of poverty.

3. Community Development

Investing in clean water access, sustainable sanitation, and structural resilience surrounding Rue Calvaire.
